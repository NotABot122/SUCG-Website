Who We Are

Seattle University's Premier Student Consulting Organization

SUCG provides students with hands-on consulting experience through real advisory projects with external organizations. We develop analytical thinking, professional communication, and structured problem-solving skills.

Students are selected through a competitive process, trained specifically for each engagement, and held to professional standards throughout. Every project creates value for the student, the client, and Seattle University.
